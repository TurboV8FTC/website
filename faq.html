How much does it cost?

There is no individual cost per person. After teams get together, all the total costs, including team registration, a robot kit of parts, and event participation ranges from about $5,000 to $6,000. Don't worry! We will get most of the money from door-to-door outreach. For more information, go to FIRST's Costs and Registrations page.

Are there any other programs we can join?

FIRST Robotics is divided into four groups. Each group is separated by a particular age group:

  • First Lego League Jr. (FLLJr) – Grades K through 4th
  • First Lego League (FLL) – Grades 4th through 8th
  • First Tech Challenge (FTC) This Club – Grades 7th through 12th
  • First Robotics Competition (FRC) – High School (9th through 12th)

Our meetings are usually from 4:30-6:30 on Saturdays. They can be hosted in the students houses. If no one is willing to host, the meeting will be in San Ramon.
