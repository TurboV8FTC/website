About Us

We are a FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) team in the Tri-Valley, California. The Tri-Valley area is comprised of San Ramon, Pleasanton, and Dublin. We consist of members from a multitude of schools around the community. In total, our team currently consists of 10 permanent members and 3 interns. For the 2019 - 2020 season, we have accepted several new interns as well!

Most of our members are from San Ramon. However, we also have two interns from the Bay Area.

Why FIRST Tech Challenge?

  • FIRST is a meaningful life experience
  • Outreach events develop confidence and public speaking skills
  • Memebrs will learn how to assemble a robot together, and in the process you can learn many hardware skills for future use.
  • All of the team members will help you improve. You may even have the chance to host a team meeting
